Introduction to Agentic Automation

Agentic Automation is an advanced form of intelligent automation where software agents can understand goals, make decisions, and perform tasks autonomously. Unlike traditional rule-based automation, it combines Artificial Intelligence, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to handle dynamic and complex business processes.

These digital agents can analyze information, plan actions, interact with applications, and continuously improve through learning. Agentic Automation enables organizations to build smarter, adaptive, and end-to-end automated workflows with minimal human intervention.

Introduction to Automation

Automation is the use of technology to perform tasks automatically with minimal human involvement. It focuses on executing repetitive and rule-based processes efficiently, improving accuracy, productivity, and operational consistency.

By leveraging tools such as Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and workflow technologies, automation helps organizations streamline operations, reduce manual effort, and focus on higher-value activities that drive innovation and business growth.
